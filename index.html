About Me

I am a trained and passionate educator with a Bachelor’s degree in Education (English & Literature). My academic background has equipped me with strong skills in curriculum interpretation, lesson planning, learner assessment, and classroom management. I have experience teaching at secondary school level, where I consistently focus on creating interactive, student-centered learning environments that encourage critical thinking and active participation.

In addition to my teaching qualification, I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Information Technology at Kabarak University. This training enables me to integrate digital tools and modern technologies into teaching and learning. I strongly believe that education in the 21st century must combine subject mastery with technological competence to prepare learners for a rapidly evolving world.

My professional interests include digital curriculum development, online learning systems, educational website development, and designing technology-supported assessment tools. I am particularly passionate about building digital solutions that simplify classroom management and improve learning efficiency.

I am committed to continuous professional growth, innovation in education, and maintaining high academic standards. My goal is to work in an institution that values both academic excellence and technology integration in teaching practice.
